Who Built This
🎣
Amber
Founder · BaitLogic™
📍 Highland / Collinsville, IL

I am a self-taught angler who got tired of showing up to the water without the right information. Every fishing app I tried was built for everyone — which means it was built for no one. So I built BaitLogic from my phone, for anglers like me, in the communities I fish.


I fish Carlyle Lake, Silver Lake, and the Mississippi River. I watch people leave trash and fishing line everywhere. I see the water quality decline. BaitLogic is my answer to all of it — community, real data, and conservation built into one free platform.

The Mission
🎣 Community First — Always Free
For the bank fisher, the beginner, the underserved angler who never had the right tools. Core features are free forever. No paywalls on fishing information. Ever.
📡 Real Local Intelligence
Live NOAA weather. USGS water temps. Community bite reports verified against real conditions. Depth charts for Carlyle, Silver Lake, Horseshoe, and the Mississippi. All in one place.
🌊 Conservation Always
The Waterway Health Index and Clean Up Crew are not features — they are the reason this exists. We are organizing anglers to protect the waters we fish before it is too late.
