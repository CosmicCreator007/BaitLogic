Honest Answers

Honest Answers

Is BaitLogic Live?
BaitLogic™ is in soft launch / early-access mode. The mission is live, the community is starting, and features are being shaped with real-user feedback. Conditions data pulls live from NOAA and USGS right now.
What Happens After I Sign Up?
You will be added to the early access list and receive updates as features roll out. No spam — ever. Complete the confirmation in your email to activate.
Who Is This For?
Anglers in St. Louis Metro and Southern Illinois — from beginners to serious anglers who want better local intel. Bank fishers, boat fishers, young anglers, veterans — everyone welcome. No experience required.
Is It Really Free?
Yes. Core features — conditions, community reports, depth data, species ID, catch log — are free forever. No paywalls. No gatekeeping. Business tools are the paid tier. Essential fishing information is never locked.
What Is Coming Next?
Full user accounts, live chat, satellite map with GPS, gear marketplace, tournament listings, invasive species reporting, push notifications for hot bites, and verified angler badges. All community-voted.
