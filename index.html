Illinois Regulations

Quick reference for Illinois anglers. Always verify with IDNR for updates.

Fishing License
Required for ages 16–64.
Daily Limits
Bass: 6 combined
Crappie: 10–25 (lake dependent)
Catfish: No statewide limit.
Size Limits
Varies by lake — many have 14" bass minimum.
Special Waters
Coffeen, Baldwin, and Kinkaid have unique rules.
