✓ Carlyle Lake — Healthy Conditions
🧪 pH Level
Most fish thrive between 6.5 and 8.5. Too low or too high destroys spawning beds.
✓ Green scum on surface = pH spike. Report it.
⚠️ Dissolved Oxygen
Below 5 mg/L fish stress. Below 3 mg/L fish die. Hot days and algae drop oxygen fast.
⚠️ Fish gasping at surface = emergency. Report immediately.
👁️ Water Clarity
Murky water blocks sunlight aquatic plants need. Below 12 inch visibility = report it.
✓ DIY: White object on string. Gone under 12in = action needed.
🚨 Fishing Line & Trash
Line takes 600 years to decompose. It kills birds, turtles, and fish. Carlyle Lake is drowning in it.
🚨 Carry a bag. Pick up 3 pieces every trip. Cut line before disposing.
🌿 Invasive Species
Asian carp, zebra mussels, and hydrilla weed are active threats. They destroy spawning habitat.
⚠️ Clean your boat and drain your livewell between waters.
🌡️ Water Temperature
Temperature drives spawn timing and feeding. Know it before you go.
✓ Bass: 60–75°F · Crappie: 55–68°F · Catfish: 70–85°F
Get Your Testing Kit

You do not need expensive equipment to be a Guardian. Basic water testing strips give you pH, dissolved oxygen, and clarity readings in seconds. Here are the best options at every price point.

🏪 Local First — Walmart / Rural King
Aquarium test strips work perfectly for basic pH and water quality checks. Found in the pet/fish section. Under $10. Pick them up next time you are already out.
Walmart → Rural King →
📦 Amazon — Best Value Kits
API 5-in-1 test strips — pH, nitrite, nitrate, carbonate hardness, and general hardness. About $12 for 25 strips. The most used kit by hobbyists and field researchers. JNW Direct also makes a solid 7-in-1 strip for under $15.
API Strips → JNW Strips →
🔬 Serious Testing — LaMotte / Hach
For Guardians who want accurate dissolved oxygen readings. LaMotte ColorQ and Hach dissolved oxygen kits are what environmental agencies use. $25–$60 but they give you the data that actually matters for fish health reporting.
LaMotte → Hach →
🌊 Free Testing — IDNR & IL EPA
Illinois EPA and IDNR offer free water quality testing for reported pollution events and fish kills. If you see something serious — dead fish, chemical smell, unusual discoloration — report it and they test for free.
Report to IL EPA → IDNR Fishing →

Clean Up Crew

Carlyle Lake is being destroyed by trash and fishing line left every season. We are organizing the people who care enough to show up.

🪢 Abandoned Fishing Line
Left on banks, in trees, and in the water every season. Kills birds, turtles, and fish. 600 years to decompose.
→ Cut line into small pieces. Never leave it on the bank.
🗑️ Trash on the Banks
Bags, containers, wrappers at access points. Enters the water and is eaten by the fish we fish for.
→ Pack it in, pack it out. Pick up 3 pieces every trip.
⚠️ Illegal Dumping
Bulk waste near boat ramps leaches chemicals directly into the lake.
→ Report: Madison/Clinton County (618) 825-2010 or IL EPA.
