Conservation & Water Health

BaitLogic treats water health as non‑negotiable. Every angler plays a role in protecting our waters.

Water Testing Instructions

  • Use test strips for pH, ammonia, nitrites, nitrates, hardness, and alkalinity
  • Dip strip 2–3 seconds, wait 30–60 seconds for color change
  • Compare to chart and log results in BaitLogic
  • Test at shoreline, mid‑depth, and near vegetation
  • Repeat weekly or after storms
