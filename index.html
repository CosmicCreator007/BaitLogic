I’m Amber — a self‑taught angler from Highland and Collinsville, Illinois. I built BaitLogic from my phone with no team, no funding, and no shortcuts because I was tired of showing up to the water without the right information.

I fish Carlyle Lake, Silver Lake, and the Mississippi River. Like a lot of local anglers, I got tired of using apps that weren’t built for our lakes, our river systems, or the way we actually fish here.

So I built the platform I wished existed.

Not another generic fishing app

Most fishing apps try to work everywhere. That usually means they don’t work well enough anywhere.

BaitLogic is different. It was built specifically for anglers in Southern Illinois and the St. Louis Metro — the people fishing Carlyle Lake, Silver Lake, the Mississippi River, and the waters around them.

Local focus isn’t a feature. It’s the whole point.

Protect the waters we fish

BaitLogic is also about stewardship. Every season, trash and fishing line get left behind at Carlyle Lake and other local waters.

The Guardian Program and Clean Up Crew bring together anglers who care enough to do something about it.
